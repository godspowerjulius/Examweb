About JAMB

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) conducts the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) for candidates seeking admission into Nigerian universities, polytechnics, and colleges of education. The exam is computer-based (CBT) with 180 questions across 4 subjects, completed in 2 hours.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is JAMB?

JAMB conducts the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) for admission into Nigerian universities, polytechnics, and colleges of education. It's a computer-based test.

How many questions are in JAMB UTME?

There are 180 questions total across 4 subjects to be answered within 2 hours. Use of English is compulsory, and you choose 3 from the remaining subjects.

What is the maximum JAMB score?

The maximum score is 400. Most universities require a minimum of 120-180 depending on your program and institution.

How do I practice for JAMB on NaijaExams?

Register an account, log in, go to the CBT setup page, select JAMB as your exam type, choose your subjects, and start practicing with timed real-exam format tests.

Can I retake the practice exam?

Yes! You can take the practice exam as many times as you want. Each attempt helps you identify weak areas and improve your performance.
