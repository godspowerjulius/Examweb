About JAMB

The Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) conducts the Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME) for candidates seeking admission into Nigerian universities, polytechnics, and colleges of education. The exam is computer-based (CBT) and consists of 4 subjects.

Format
